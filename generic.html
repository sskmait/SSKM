shri Swami Krishananand Mahavidyalay

shri Swami Krishananand Mahavidyalay College, established in 2017, offers a dynamic academic environment with three degree programs: Bachelor of Science in Education (BSE), Diploma in Elementary Education (D.El.Ed), and Bachelor of Arts (BA). Focused on fostering comprehensive education, the college aims to equip students with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills, preparing them for successful careers in education and the arts. With experienced faculty and a commitment to quality education, shri Swami Krishananand Mahavidyalay College is dedicated to developing future educators and scholars.

About the faculty

Shri Swami Krishananand Mahavidyalay boasts a dedicated team of experienced faculty members who are passionate about teaching and mentorship. The faculty's expertise spans various fields, ensuring that students receive quality education and guidance. .

About the infrastructure

The college is equipped with modern infrastructure, including well-equipped classrooms, libraries with extensive collections, and technology resources that facilitate effective learning. Collaborative spaces encourage student interaction and group work, fostering a vibrant academic community
