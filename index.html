Shri Swami Krishananand Mahavidyalay

Shri Swami Krishananand Mahavidyalay College, established in 1993, offers a dynamic academic environment with four degree programs: Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.), Diploma in Elementary Education (D.El.Ed), Bachelor of Arts (BA), and Basic Teaching Course (BTC). Focused on fostering comprehensive education, the college aims to equip students with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills, preparing them for successful careers in education and various fields of study. With experienced faculty and a commitment to quality education, Shri Swami Krishananand Mahavidyalay College is dedicated to developing future educators, scholars, and professionals.
